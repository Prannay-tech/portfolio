About Me

Hey there! I'm Prannay, a data science professional with a passion for turning complex data into actionable insights. I recently completed my MS in Business Analytics and AI from the University of Texas at Dallas, where I discovered my love for bridging the gap between technical analysis and business strategy.

My journey started with a simple curiosity about how data can tell stories and drive decisions. Today, I specialize in machine learning, predictive analytics, and building intelligent solutions that actually make a difference. From developing advanced chatbots to creating disease prediction models with 99% accuracy, I love tackling challenging problems that have real-world impact.

As a business analyst at heart, I excel at translating technical findings into clear business recommendations. Whether it's identifying market trends, optimizing operational processes, or helping stakeholders make data-driven decisions, I thrive on making complex analytics accessible and actionable.

When I'm not diving deep into datasets or building models, you'll find me exploring new technologies, contributing to open-source projects, or sharing insights through my research and publications. I believe the best solutions come from combining technical expertise with a deep understanding of business needs.

Outside of work, I love listening to music, exploring new places to travel to, trekking through nature trails, and watching movies. These hobbies help me stay balanced and bring fresh perspectives to my analytical work.
