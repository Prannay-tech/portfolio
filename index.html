About Me

I'm an AI Engineer and Data Scientist with an MS in Business Analytics and AI from the University of Texas at Dallas. I specialize in building production-grade AI systems — from Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) pipelines and LLM-powered applications to scalable data engineering workflows that process millions of records.

My engineering work spans the full AI/data stack: designing vector search architectures with FAISS and ChromaDB, orchestrating data pipelines with PySpark and Snowflake, deploying ML models with XGBoost and Neural Networks, and building NLP systems including published research in Automatic Speech Recognition. I've reduced loan default rates by 88.4%, improved chatbot accuracy by 85%, and achieved 99% disease prediction accuracy in real-world applications.

I have a strong foundation in the full data engineering stack — from raw ingestion and transformation to serving insights through interactive BI dashboards in Power BI and Tableau. I believe great AI products are built on great data infrastructure, and I'm passionate about both sides of that equation.

Outside of engineering, I love listening to music, traveling, trekking, and watching films — hobbies that keep me curious and bring fresh perspective to analytical thinking.
